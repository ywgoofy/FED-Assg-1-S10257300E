About Us

We are Kessoku Band, an all-girl rock band from Tokyo, Japan. We are made up of Hitori "Bocchi" Gotō (guitar and vocals), Nijika Ijichi (drums), and Ryō Yamada (bass). We started the band in high school because we all shared a love of music. We are still learning and growing as musicians, but we are passionate about making music that we love. We hope that you will enjoy our music!
